## How billing works

Billing is hourly for each resource (app, database). The meter starts when the container starts and stops when it is deleted or stopped.

Each Pro organization receives **R$20/month** in credits automatically applied to the invoice. Unused credits do not roll over to the next month.

The final cost is calculated as:

```
cost = active_hours × tier_hourly_price
```

## Compute tiers

Each app and database runs on a compute tier that defines available RAM, CPU, and disk.

| Tier | RAM | vCPU | Disk | Price/hour |
|------|-----|------|------|-----------|
| `NANO` | 512 MB | 0.5 | 5 GB | Free |
| `SMALL` | 1 GB | 1 | 10 GB | R$0,02 |
| `MEDIUM` | 2 GB | 1.5 | 20 GB | R$0,04 |
| `LARGE` | 4 GB | 2 | 40 GB | R$0,08 |
| `XLARGE` | 8 GB | 4 | 80 GB | R$0,16 |

<Note>
  The NANO tier is free and intended for testing. Apps on NANO sleep after 15 minutes of inactivity, even on the Pro plan.
</Note>

## Auth MAU

Nivo Auth counts MAU by unique users active in the month. Usage is visible on the project compute page.

| Plan | Included | Overage |
|------|----------|---------|
| Free | 500 MAU | No overage |
| Pro | 10,000 MAU | R$0.008 per MAU above the limit |

On Pro, Auth overage uses monthly credits before creating additional charges.

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Does the Free plan allow creating apps?">
    No. The Free plan has no compute tiers enabled. Upgrade to Pro to create apps and databases.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens if I exceed R$20 of usage on Pro?">
    The overage is charged to the registered card at the end of the month. You can monitor usage in real time in the dashboard.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I cancel at any time?">
    Yes. Cancellation is immediate. You are not charged for unused time after cancellation.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do apps sleep on the Pro plan?">
    Only apps on the NANO tier. All other tiers stay always active on Pro.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
